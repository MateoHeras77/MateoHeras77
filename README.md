## <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/hvRJCLFzcasrR4ia7z/giphy.gif" width="28"> &nbsp; About me

I'm a **Data Analyst** working at **Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ)** — the busiest airport in Canada.
My day-to-day is turning messy ground-handling data into **Power BI dashboards, ETL pipelines and forecasting models** that help airlines land, turn and push back on time.

- ✈️ Currently at **PrimeFlight Aviation Services** — building analytics for aircraft turnarounds, grooming compliance & staffing.
- 🎓 Finishing my **MSc in Data Analytics** at the University of Niagara Falls (March 2026).
- 📈 Background in **economics + econometrics** (R, Stata, panel data, time series). Published in a Scopus Q2 journal ([Amenities & Crime](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rspp.2024.100062)).
- 🧪 Obsessed with the intersection of **operations ↔ machine learning** — the boring problems where small improvements mean big money.
- 🛫 Holder of a **Purple RAIC** — I don't just analyze the airside, I walk it.
- 🌎 Ecuadorian in Canada. I speak Spanish, English and data.
